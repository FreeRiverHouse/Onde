## Key learnings for future prompts:
1. **Ask Grok to optimize prompts FOR Grok/Flux** - not generic prompts
2. **Natural, detailed descriptive language** works better than keywords
3. **Consistent character description** throughout (middle-aged, short beard, curly hair)
4. **Quality stack at the end** (museum quality, Rembrandt/Caravaggio, 8k, chiaroscuro)
5. **Emotional atmosphere** described explicitly
