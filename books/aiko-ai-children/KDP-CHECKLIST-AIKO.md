## Descrizione Libro (per KDP - max 4000 caratteri)

```
What is AI? How does it learn? And why should kids care?

When seven-year-old Sofia discovers a small, round robot named AIKO on her birthday, she embarks on a journey to understand one of the most important technologies of our time: Artificial Intelligence.

Through gentle conversations with her new robot friend, Sofia learns:
- What AI really is (hint: it's like a super-powered recipe!)
- How AI learns to see and recognize things
- How AI learned to talk by reading millions of books
- What AI can do well - and what it cannot do
- Four important rules for using AI safely
- Why the future depends on kids like her

AIKO doesn't just explain technology - it helps children develop a healthy, curious relationship with AI. In a world where children interact with smart devices daily, this book gives them the vocabulary and understanding to ask better questions.

Perfect for:
- Curious kids who ask "But HOW does it work?"
- Parents looking for age-appropriate tech education
- Teachers introducing technology concepts
- Families who want to discuss AI together

Written in simple, engaging language with beautiful watercolor illustrations, AIKO makes complex concepts accessible without oversimplifying. The book emphasizes that AI is a powerful tool - but humans are always in charge.

Includes a special "Note for Parents and Teachers" with key AI concepts explained.

From Onde Publishing - Stories that help children understand their world.
```
