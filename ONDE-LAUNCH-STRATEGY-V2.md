## Slide 3: Brand Identity

### üé® Visual Language

**Color Palette:**
- Dorato Onde: #d4af37 (primary, accents)
- Dorato Chiaro: #f5e6b3 (backgrounds, highlights)
- Crema Caldo: #faf8f5 (page backgrounds)
- Testo Scuro: #2c3e50 (titles, body text)

**Illustration Style:**
- European watercolor technique
- 1950s-60s Italian aesthetic
- Beatrix Potter meets Luzzati meets Munari
- Visible brushstrokes, warm golden light
- Natural skin tones, NO rosy cheeks

**Typography:**
- Serif fonts for titles (classic elegance)
- Sans-serif for body text (modern readability)
- Consistent hierarchy across all materials
