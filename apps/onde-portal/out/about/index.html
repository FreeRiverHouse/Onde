Our Story

From Italy to Los Angeles

Onde was born from a simple dream: to create books that children want to read again, and again. Books that smell of paper and colors, but speak the language of the future.

We are a small team scattered between Italy and California, united by a passion for beautiful stories and the belief that technology, used wisely, can amplify human creativity.

Each of our books combines the art of traditional illustration - watercolors, shading, golden light - with digital possibilities. The result? Stories that live on paper and screen, that grow with the children who read them.
