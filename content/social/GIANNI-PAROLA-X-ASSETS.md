# Gianni Parola - X Account Assets

Prompt e specifiche per creare gli asset del profilo X di @GianniParola.

---

## 1. Foto Profilo (400x400)

### Prompt Grok

```
European watercolor children's book illustration style,
portrait of Italian male writer in his 40s named Gianni Parola,
curly dark hair, round glasses, brown tweed jacket,
warm intellectual appearance, holding a fountain pen,
gentle smile, welcoming expression,
soft brushstrokes, warm golden light,
cozy study background with blurred books,
Beatrix Potter aesthetic, whimsical but refined,
square format, centered face, suitable for profile picture,
NOT photo realistic, NOT 3D, NOT Pixar,
natural skin tone NO rosy cheeks, 4k
```

### Specifiche
- Dimensioni: 400x400px (quadrato)
- Formato: JPG o PNG
- Il viso deve essere centrato
- Background sfocato per non distrarre

### File Output
- `gianni-parola-avatar.jpg`
- Path: `/assets/social/gianni-parola/`

## 2. Banner/Header (1500x500)

### Prompt Grok

```
Wide panoramic banner illustration, European watercolor style,
cozy Italian writer's study, wooden desk with manuscripts and ink,
stack of children's books with colorful spines,
warm golden afternoon light through window,
antique lamp, scattered papers, fountain pen on desk,
bookshelf in background, potted plants,
Beatrix Potter meets Luzzati aesthetic,
soft brushstrokes, warm amber and cream tones,
NOT photo realistic, NOT 3D, NOT Pixar,
1500x500 banner format, 4k
```

### Specifiche
- Dimensioni: 1500x500px
- Formato: JPG
- Lo spazio per la foto profilo (sinistra) deve essere considerato
- Toni caldi, accoglienti

### File Output
- `gianni-parola-banner.jpg`
- Path: `/assets/social/gianni-parola/`

## 5. Workflow Generazione

1. Vai su `x.com/i/grok`
2. Clicca "Create Images"
3. Incolla il prompt per Avatar
4. Genera → Upscale → Scarica
5. Ripeti per Banner
6. Salva in `/assets/social/gianni-parola/`
7. Manda su Telegram per approvazione
